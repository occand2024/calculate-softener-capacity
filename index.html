Industrial Softener Capacity Calculator

Calculates softener throughput and regeneration interval with a safety factor (Capacity × (1 − Safety%)) to reduce hardness breakthrough risk.

Inputs

If you have ppm, divide by 17.1 to get gpg.
Adds 3 gpg per 1 ppm Fe to effective hardness.
Adds 2 gpg per 1 ppm Mn to effective hardness.
Default 10% (e.g., 26,087 × 0.90 = 23,478 gallons).
Optional extra derate beyond safety (e.g., outages, variability).
